योजनाकार-Yojanakar(Planner) is an event organising and scheduling tool that automates the work of an corporate event organiser, reducing his/her burden in the areas of planning, recording, scheduling and rescheduling. It also provides a view of the event fixtures for the general public.

Game event orgainsers, especially in corporate offices spend much of their time in fixing the correct opponents in the first round (Really!!! Believe me :)) For example, in a knock out event, the top seeded players should not be the direct opponents in the first round. Think of Federer vs Djokovic in the first round.

Similarly the other end of the lane must also be provided with an opportuninty to be played with a ranked player.

योजनाकार uses a complex algorithm to schedule events based on "Yojanakar Expertise Transformed Index" (YETI). Each player's expertise, experience, fitness and popularity are used to create YETI.
